Spatial distribution map of variation in ΔPb208 in Icelandic rock samples

This map was complied in ArcGIS using spatial interpretation of data points collated together by the georoc database ad my own data. The data is from basaltic samples only, since we wanted to measure the variation in Pb208 in the Icelandic mantle with as little crustal contamination as possible. The map shows exactly what one would expect — a lower ΔPb208 in the south west than in the north, and the highest values restricted to the areas around the proposed mantle plume and where the deepest melting is occuring beneath Öræfajökull.
